ACM is the world's largest education and scientific computing society. It delivers resources that advance computing as a science and a profession. ACM provides the computing field's premier Digital Library and serves its members and the computing profession with leading-edge publications, conferences, and career resources.

Words from our President: "My goal of the ACM is to instill students with a sense of self efficacy through partaking in extracurricular projects pertaining to computer science, providing them with the confidence and competitive edge upon graduation, ultimately making them more desirable to prospective employment opportunities."
